# What the PDF contains

## 1. Header / Identifier
- Page numbers
- Sender/company heading such as:
  - "Tanesha Reed | East Coast Incorporations"
- SMSF/client information appears inside the email body:
  - "Establish SMSF: Sulianas SMSF"

NOT a dedicated formatted header like:
- SMSF Name
- Company Name
- Client Summary

The PDF looks more like a raw Outlook print/export.

---

## 2. Separate Email Sections
Yes.

Each email section contains:
- From
- Sent
- To
- Cc (if applicable)
- Subject
- Body

Structure resembles:

```text
EMAIL 1
From:
Sent:
To:
Subject:
Body

EMAIL 2
...
```

---

## 3. Email Threads / Replies
Yes.

The PDF preserves:
- reply chains
- quoted emails
- nested Outlook conversation history

Meaning:
- earlier emails are repeated inside replies
- conversation context is retained

---

## 4. Ordering
The emails are NOT consistently chronological.

Observed order:
- Dec 3, 2025
- Oct 23, 2025
- Oct 30, 2025

So the PDF appears to follow:
- Outlook selection order
or
- manual export order

NOT:
- oldest → newest
- newest → oldest

---

## 5. Branding / Formatting
Minimal formatting only.

Includes:
- Outlook print layout
- email signatures
- legal disclaimers
- plain text formatting

Does NOT include:
- logos
- branded cover pages
- custom styling
- accounting-firm formatting
- table of contents
